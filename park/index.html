Discover the Wild Side of Nairobi

Established in 1946, Nairobi National Park is Kenya's first national park in the world that exists within a capital city's limits. Despite its proximity to urban development, the park remains home to a diverse range of wildlife including lions, giraffes, zebras, and over 400 bird species.

Park Highlights

Wildlife Safari

Experience close encounters with Africa's iconic wildlife including lions, rhinos, buffaloes, and various antelope species.

Bird Watching

With over 400 bird species, the park is a paradise for bird enthusiasts and photographers.

Scenic Views

Enjoy breathtaking landscapes with the unique backdrop of Nairobi's skyline contrasting with the natural savanna.
